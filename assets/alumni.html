SUPPORT THE ENDOWMENT

We are making significant progress towards reaching our initial $50,000 goal to start the endowment. This fund would ensure a consistent stream of funding for the team that would enable long-term equipment planning and ensure future team success. We hope you will join us in solidifying the Rowing Club's presences on campus by donating HERE. Gift designation is pre-selected on the donations page. All gifts are tax-deductible contributions to Carnegie Mellon University and are designated directly to the Tartan Crew Alumni Network Endowed Fund.
Please see our one-pager or slide deck for more information on the endowment.

How to make a donation through CMU's donations portal:
On the CMU donation page, select "Search All Funds" and search for "Tartan Crew Alumni Network Endowed Fund".
